About Me

There are some folks who like to read long biographies, and others who prefer three short words to describe someone. For those who fall into the latter category, this is for you: Programmer. Explorer. Curious.

For those in the former, the following is all yours: As with all people, there is of certainly more to my story, but if I had only three words to choose, it would be the aforementioned. Certainly the most important of those would be "Curious." Were it not for my lifelong curiousity, I would likely not be able to use the other two words in my description. Curiousity drove me at a very young age to begin exploring the forests near my house. Later it drove me to explore mountains, and utlimately to exploring rivers. Each of these paths provided great trials, greater rewards, and ultimately a plethora of learning.

The same curiousity that pushed me into the outdoors also led me to programming. After spending a few years in tech sales at Best Buy and then Apple, along with twice as many years in the outdoor industry starting in sales, moving into management, and then into content creation and marketing, I found my curiousity no longer satisfied. These fields had very finite amounts of knowledge to be uncovered, and so I sought a field where I might be able to truly spend the rest of my career constantly learning, therefore constantly satiating my natural curiosity. Between research online, friends, and a small bit of experience, all paths pointed towards programming.

I began my path towards my programming career by enrolling at Epicodus, where I started the C# and React course in January of 2022...
